Welcome to the Pokemart Planner

     

◓ Overview ◓

The PokéMart Budget Planner is a web-based tool designed to help Pokémon trainers efficiently manage their spending on essential supplies! Inspired by the classic Pokémon gameplay experience, this application allows trainers to track their purchases, monitor their budget, and maintain financial discipline while stocking up on items for their journey. All transactions are calculated in PokeDollars (₽)

This application not only serves as a practical tool for Pokémon gameplay but also introduces fundamental concepts of:
1)Budget management and expense tracking
2)Web development with client-side data persistence
3)User interface design and form handling
4)Real-time calculations and data updates

The PokéMart Budget Planner successfully combines nostalgic Pokémon elements with practical budgeting functionality, creating an engaging tool that enhances the gaming experience while promoting responsible spending habits in the Pokémon universe.
